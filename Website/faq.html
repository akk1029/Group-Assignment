Our Team

In 2023, 5 friends in college shared an interest in technology and entrepreneurship and wished to share their passion for it with the world, this led up to the founding of ByteBay. The success of the company required them to use all their skills and talents to its fullest, such as coding the website, forming relationships with big tech companies and earning customers trust and faith in them. Many sleepless night and countless hours were poured into ByteBay. Today we are able to stand proud and say it was all worth it. This is all thanks to the unending support from our customers and employees.
